Why ?

For every old people, it's important to have a list of medication so they must not forget about what they have to take, with wich dosage etc. So in this application, they will be able to see this list created by the caregiver beforehand for every patient.

List of medication

When the patient will want to see the list of medication, there is this display with the name, the quantity and the dosing time. For the caregivers, they will have a bonus button who will permit to change the list (delete,add) if the patient have a new treatment.
